About Us

Our Mission

Pentecostal Gospel Church is a vibrant community of believers dedicated to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ. We are committed to worship, fellowship, discipleship, and service.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and transformation in Area 50 and beyond, empowering individuals to live purpose-driven lives through the power of the Holy Spirit.

Our Values

  • Faith in Jesus Christ
  • Spirit-led Worship
  • Biblical Teaching
  • Community & Fellowship
  • Service & Outreach
